About The Hub

More Than Just
A Building.

We are iLearn Spaces Youth Hub: a social innovation centre on a mission to champion and strengthen the potential of young people in Gwagwalada and the wider Abuja area. We do this by providing a world-class environment where young creatives, tech enthusiasts, and entrepreneurs can access the infrastructure, skills, and networks they need to succeed. We are more than just a building; we are a movement. Powered by RABNI Education Interventions, we work with our partners to bridge the gap between education and the global economy, ensuring that your location is never a barrier to your opportunity.
