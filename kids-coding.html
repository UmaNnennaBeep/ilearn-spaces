Frequently Asked Questions

My child has never coded before. Is that okay?

Absolutely! Our "Kids Tech Bootcamp" is designed exactly for beginners. We start with visual blocks and fun games.

What equipment do we need?

A laptop or desktop, a stable internet connection, and a curiosity to learn! Tablets can work for some introductory courses.

What if we miss a class?

No stress. We provide catch-up materials so your child never falls behind, and sessions are often recorded.
