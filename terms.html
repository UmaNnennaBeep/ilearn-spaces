1. Code of Conduct

All members, students, and visitors at iLearn Spaces are expected to:

  • Treat all staff and peers with respect. Bullying, harassment, or discrimination of any kind will result in immediate expulsion without refund.
  • Use hub resources responsibly.
  • Maintain a quiet and professional atmosphere in co-working zones.

2. Payments & Refunds

Courses

Course fees must be paid in full before the start date. Refunds are only available if a cancellation request is made at least 7 days prior to the course start date. No refunds will be issued once a course has commenced.
